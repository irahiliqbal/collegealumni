Working throughout the year to build relationships between students and alumni.

The College Alumni Network,TCAN is one of the largest student run organizations on campus today. It is committed to connecting students to College alumni and the larger community through a variety of programs and events throughout the year. TCAN acts as ambassadors at College functions and help facilitate connection between students and alumni.

TCAN plays a huge role during Placements. Members of TCAN also benefit from the great leadership skills they learn, and they receive a behind the scenes look at many of our top alumni. Members have the opportunity to interact with an array of alumni and gain great networking skills hosting alumni events as student ambassadors.

Meet our members




Facilitating Alumni & Student Interaction

TCAN does a great job of hosting events that instill pride in the student body and teach them the history of the college. TCAN also runs an alumni speaker series that has been hugely successful in bringing out students to alumni-sponsored talks, and hearing directly from industry leaders who also went to Union. The speaker series has been a great engagement piece for the alumni to reconnect with current students and share their own Union story and how they might learn from that. .

Fundraising

A great example of how TCAN helped bridge the gap between students and alumni is participating in the college's 24-hour fundraising challenge. In 2022 they helped table all day long in the campus center, and engaged directly with their student peers about why they should support the college.

Fostering relationships

TCAN provides great feedback about ideas that College Relations has about engaging new graduates. For our Seniors, I often use them as a sounding board about how to engage them and what they'd like to see when they graduate. I've found they are very candid about what they like, and will certainly let you know if something won't work.
